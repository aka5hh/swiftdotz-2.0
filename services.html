1. Industrial Food Ingredients & Processed Ingredients

Sucrose & Refined Sugar

Refined sugar, also known as sucrose, is a highly purified form of sugar produced through an advanced refining process that removes impurities, color and minerals from raw sugar. It is widely used in the food and beverage industry because of its consistent quality, standardized sweetness and high purity level. Refined sugar is commonly used in:

  • Food processing & beverage production
  • Confectionery & baking
  • Dairy manufacturing

Food Grade Ingredients

Food grade ingredients are raw materials and additives that meet strict safety, hygiene and regulatory standards for food production. These ingredients are processed and certified to be safe for human consumption and are widely used by food manufacturers and processors.

Common products include:

  • Food additives and stabilizers
  • Sweeteners and flavor enhancers
  • Processing ingredients for food manufacturing
  • Preservatives and functional ingredients
